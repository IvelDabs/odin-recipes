Rump teak

Enjoy a juicy rump steak, topped with creamy mushrooms for a quick and easy 15 minute meal. This yummy mouth watering meal at a serve contains 512 calories

It may be wise to note that if you plan on trying out this amazing recipe you should keep in mind the following: Recipe may contain gluten, wheat, milk, lactose and fish.And as a pro tip: You can prep the onions and parsley while the steaks and potatoes are cooking in the background.

INGREDENTS:

  • 600g beef rump steak, cut into 4 portio
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • Baby rocket leaves, to serve
  • Parsley, finely chopped, to serve
  • 500g baby potatoes, microwave-ready
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 brown onion, finely diced
  • 200g sliced button mushrooms

STEPS IN PREPARATION OF RUMP STAKE

  1. Step 1 Place bag of potatoes into microwave, as per packet instructions and microwave for 6 to 8 minutes, or until tender. Set aside covered with foil to keep warm. Drizzle with a little oil and season with salt and pepper, to serve.
  2. Step 2 Meanwhile, brush steaks with 1 tablespoon oil and season with salt and pepper. Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Add steaks and cook for 3 to 4 minutes each side or until cooked to your liking. Transfer to a plate and cover with foil.
  3. Step 3 Increase temperature to medium-high. Add onion and mushrooms and cook for 4 to 5 minutes or until golden. Add Worcestershire sauce, sour cream and 1/3 cup water. Bring to the boil and remove from the heat. Season with salt and pepper. Serve steaks topped with mushroom sauce, baby potatoes sprinkled with parsley, and baby rocket on the side.
